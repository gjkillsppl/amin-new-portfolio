UX DESIGN | UI DESIGN | DEV

Anahuac Mayab
University

Role: UI/UX Lead + Frontend Development Duration: 18 Months
Tools: Sketch, Figma, Miro, CSS, JS, Hubspot CMS and more.

The Anahuac Mayab University is a private institution of higher education located in Mérida México. It is the city's most prestigious institution with more than 4000 current students it's the second most important university of International the Anahuac University Network.

TL;DR

Improved Information Architecture, helped increase direct traffic by 89.42 %, and contact creation increased by 209.67%, saved uncountable hours of manual work and improved brand consistency.

The problem

I've been working here for two years as a UI/UX Designer and Developer. As soon as I started working here, I started noticing inconsistencies in how brand assets were being used, students complaining about the information architecture of the site and how there was no site search functionality, the website didn't follow the brand guidelines; colors, typography and components were being used incorrectly.


The Solution

Information Architecture

While tackling the information architecture I started to understand that the grouping in the navigation was more related to how the business was ran and less about how students might perceive it, thru card sorting we started improving the sitemap and creating better taxonomies for the navigation.

Card Sorting

We Did some tests with students and employees of the organization that help us find out how they perceived the different entities of the University.

Sitemap

For this specific project we used Flowmapp to helps us create and receive feedback and comments from the stakeholders.

User Interviews

Conducted Interviews with students and employees to understand their needs and empathize with their stories.

A site search was also missing and as soon as it was implemented visitors started using it right away and were using it more than the navigation itself since some things were missing from it, even tho it's not as used as it was before is still one of the more clicked elements on the website as expected.

Design System

Another challenge that was evident from the start was that brand consistency had to be enforced and that content editors were reaching a variety of components to achieve the same task and that these components were too open and had too many options for them to get lost and make mistakes. The company just had a rebranding a year before I started working here. Colors changed, typography changed, the agency that did the rebranding introduced a new illustration system, and there were no guidelines on how to use the brand in a digital environment.


I began designing and prototyping some components in Figma and started coding them as modules on Hubspot CMS. At first, I had to explain to management why I was spending more time than they were accustomed to doing mundane daily tasks and I was trying to pitch the idea of a design system, The team was skeptical at first since they were not familiar with Design Systems. I received resistance from coworkers but after a few months of experiencing the benefits of reusable components, management got onboard with the project. During the first four months, I designed +25 reusable components.

As management started seeing the fruits of the elements we had created and how it improved consistency and saved us time. I was assigned with leading the project. They hired an extra UI Designer and are currently looking for an extra UI developer to work exclusively on this project. They are planning to pitch the idea of a unified design system for all the universities of the network.

Automation

When I joined the team the process of updating everything was done manually, for example; if a program or an event appeared on 5 different website pages they would go and spend hours and hours and this led to a lot of pages with outdated information.


With the help of the relational data storage system in Hubspot CMS (HubDB) I created some tables that could help us to be a single source of truth and by just updating here it would update in every single instance where this data was displayed, saving the company of hours and hours of manual work.

Results

The year after we began implementing these changes, these changes helped increase the following KPI's. Direct traffic increased by 89.42 %, and contact creation increased by 209.67%
