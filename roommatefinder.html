Roommate Finder

Role: UI/UX Lead Duration: 1 Week
Tools: Figma, Miro, Airtable, Illustrator, PS. Deliverables: Competitive Analysis, Online Survey, CJM, Flowchart, User Personas, MoSCoW Chart, High Fidelity Mockups, Prototypes.

Roommate Finder is a mobile app that connects homeowners with like-minded roommates in a safe and intellligent way.

Design Brief

Design a mobile product experience that appeals to millennials that makes it safe to find the ideal roommate in Mexico City. The experience is designed from the perspective of a person who is looking for a roommate as well as an apartment. Once the ideal roommate is found, what else can this product do to make the roommate experience better?


The Problem

54% of the world’ s population live in urban communities, the average marriage age for men is 29 (up from 26 two decades ago) and for women is 27 (up from 23 in the same time period). Given these trends, city dwellers tend to spend most of their twenties living with roommates. Finding and keeping a good roommate, however, gets harder as more people swarm into cities.

User Research

Given the timeframe I had for the project, I had to do some trade-offs in the user research phase, but I wanted to validate the original problem. I determined that the quickest way to discover the most prevalent pain points in people looking for a roommate or a place to live would be through an online survey. The survey consisted of open questions to get the most insights from the participants and then grouped them according to topics to identify trends.
